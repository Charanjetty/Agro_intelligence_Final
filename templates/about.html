Our Story

Built for Farmers, By Agricultural Experts

AgroIntelligence was born from a simple observation: farmers in Andhra Pradesh needed better tools to make informed decisions about their crops. Traditional methods of crop selection often relied on guesswork or outdated information.

Our team of agricultural scientists, data engineers, and software developers came together to create a platform that democratizes access to advanced agricultural intelligence. We've trained our AI model on thousands of data points from across all 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh.
