Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgroIntelligence and how to use our platform effectively.

General Questions

What is AgroIntelligence?

AgroIntelligence is an AI-powered agricultural platform designed for Andhra Pradesh farmers. We provide personalized crop recommendations, fertilizer plans, irrigation guidance, real-time weather updates, and information about government schemes—all in one place.

Is AgroIntelligence free to use?

Yes! AgroIntelligence is completely free for all farmers in Andhra Pradesh. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced agricultural intelligence and help farmers make better decisions.

Which districts are covered?

We cover all 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh, including Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, Guntur, Tirupati, Anantapuram, Kurnool, and many more. Our AI model has been trained on data from across the entire state.

Can I use this on my mobile phone?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Simply visit our website from any device with an internet connection.

Using the Platform

What's the difference between Manual Mode and Auto Mode?

Manual Mode: You provide your own soil test data (pH, NPK values, organic carbon). This gives the most accurate recommendations if you have recent soil test results.

Auto Mode: We use district-level averages from our database. Just select your district and season, and we'll auto-fill typical soil values. This is great if you don't have soil test data.

How accurate are the crop recommendations?

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of data points from all 26 districts and achieves over 85% accuracy in predicting suitable crops. However, recommendations are advisory—actual results depend on many factors including farming practices, pest management, and market conditions.

Where can I get soil testing done?

You can get soil testing done at:

  • Rythu Bharosa Kendrams (RBKs) in your mandal
  • Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs)
  • Agricultural universities and research stations
  • Private soil testing laboratories
Most RBKs offer free or subsidized soil testing services.

How often should I check weather updates?

We recommend checking weather daily, especially before:

  • Planning irrigation activities
  • Applying pesticides or fungicides
  • Harvesting operations
  • Sowing new crops
Our weather data is updated in real-time from Open-Meteo.

Can I change the language?

Yes! On the home page dashboard, you'll find language toggle buttons at the top. You can switch between English and Telugu (తెలుగు) at any time. The interface will update immediately.

Technical Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No! AgroIntelligence currently doesn't require user registration. You can access all features immediately without creating an account. We may add optional user accounts in the future for saving your preferences and history.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We take data security seriously. We don't sell your information, and any data you provide is used only to generate recommendations and improve our services. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

What if the website isn't working?

Try these steps:

  • Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Check your internet connection
If problems persist, contact us at support@agrointelligence.in

Can I use this offline?

Currently, AgroIntelligence requires an internet connection to access real-time weather data and generate AI-powered recommendations. We're exploring offline capabilities for future versions.

Government Schemes

How do I apply for government schemes?

Visit our Schemes page to browse available programs. Each scheme card has a "Visit portal" button that takes you to the official application website. You can also apply in person at your local Rythu Bharosa Kendram with required documents (Aadhaar, land records, bank passbook).

What documents do I need for scheme applications?

Commonly required documents include:

  • Aadhaar card
  • Land ownership documents (Pattadar passbook)
  • Bank account details and passbook
  • Soil health card (for some schemes)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
Specific requirements vary by scheme.

Are the scheme links official?

Yes! All scheme links on our platform direct you to official government portals. We regularly update these links to ensure they're current and accurate.
